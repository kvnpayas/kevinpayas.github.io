Festivals in the Philippines

Festivals are incredible in the Philippines and this is one of the reasons why It’s More Fun to visit every single island. Often including elaborate costumes, parades and processions, and demonstrating the integration of historical traditions from the indigenous as well as Spanish and Mexican influences that characterize modern Philippines.

Festivals is part and bundle of Filipino culture. Through good times and bad times, the feast must go on. Each city and barrio has at least one local festival of its own, usually on the feast of its patron saint, so that there is always a fiesta going on somewhere in the country. But the major and most elaborate festival of all is Christmas, a season celebrated with all the display and show the fun-loving Filipino can handle.
